Change in Roster Arrangement for Alliance Quests

Posted on July 26, 2016

Due to the fact that we have been unsuccessful to 100% alliance quests recently, the officers and I have decided to change the way the roster is organized.

Veteran players, or players who are shown to be more active/skillful, will now be designated to the first 2 groups of AQ. Newer/not so active players will be designated to group 3.

By having our top players in the 2 groups, we greatly increase our chances of receiving Alliance Quest crystals for all members and getting a chance to get T4 catalysts, which we all love and pray for.

If you find that you are in group 3, it is not meant as a form of punishment. It is meant to prove your worth to this alliance. We still expect you to perform your best just as we would with any member. It does not give you an excuse to be lazy and not participate. If you don’t like it, then feel free to leave any time.

Alternatively, members in groups 1 & 2 that are found to be lacking in participation/skill, will be bumped down to group 3, giving a group 3 member a chance to be in the top 2 groups.

Our hope is to someday 100% all 3 Alliance Quests in all 3 groups. This will show us that this alliance is moving on up to the east side. Which, in turn, will allow us to open 3 groups for war, helping guarantee all members a chance to win more shards.

Until that day comes though, this is the way it will be for now.

Thank you.

Participation in AQ for 3 War Groups

Posted on July 7, 2016

We realize that now that we have started to move up in rank for war, people may want to join war, but can't, because slots have been taken up.

We want to open up all 3 slots in war, but we will only do so until we get almost full participation in all Alliance Quests.

If we can't get full participation in Alliance Quests, then it's a sign we won't get full participation in Alliance Wars, thus making us lose a war and missing out on winning shards.

So until we start getting full participation in all Aliiance Quests consitently, we will continue to do 2 groups for war until further notice.

Thank you.
