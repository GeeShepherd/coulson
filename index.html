Hello AQ 5!

Posted on January 17, 2017

Map 5 will now be added to our weekly schedule for AQ and will occur every Tuesday while war is shut down.

----------DONATIONS----------

With the inclusion of map 5 to our weekly schedule, we are also going to start requiring donations from all members. We will now require all members to donate all gold and battlechips won from Alliance Quests into the alliance treasury. The officers and I agree that donating AQ rewards would be the easiest for the alliance since they are an automatic given for AQs win or lose. It also doesn’t require you to grind in arenas just to donate to the treasury. We don’t want to make this game more of a grind than it has to be.

We will also require a minimum of 2.5k loyalty donated to the treasury a week as well. Again, this is a small amount that is required. We win about 4k loyalty minimum just for losing a war. So this weekly minimum shouldn’t put you out too much.

Of course if you wish to donate more than the minimum, that will of course be helpful, but it is not required nor expected.

------------TIME------------

With the inclusion of map 5. Time will be of the essence. We had it easy when it was free during the Holiday season with the 30 minute timers. But now that we are back on the 1 hour timers, we will have to be extra attentive. We will need all of you to bring you’re A game when we do map 5. Because we all want those precious map 5 crystals and those even more precious T4CCs.

-----------ROSTER-----------

The Map 5 roster may change frequently due to busy schedules. We know some of you have busy lives/school/work schedules. So with that in mind, group 3 will be our ‘relaxed’ group. We do not expect you to finish, but we do still expect you to try your hardest to explore when online. Because the more points we get, the more milestones we will reach.

If you are going to be on vacation or think will not be able to perform well in map 5 due to time restraints. Please let us know and we will try to adjust accordingly.

Thank you all. And good luck to us!

Rewards!

Posted on August 24, 2016

We are excited to announce that we will now be rewarding our top players! The officers and I will be keeping an eye on players who really stand out and who we consider the MVP of the award season (A period ranging from 2-4 weeks). Some of the things we look for in an MVP is that they:

  • Perform well in AQ
  • Perform well in AW
  • Have the best fighting style (Can keep their health high)

To keep things fair, award winners will have to wait a certain length of time before they can qualify for MVP again for an award season. This grants more of our top players the chance to win rewards instead of the same player getting rewarded repeatedly.

Officers will also be excluded from winning rewards to remove any suspicion of foul play. This will be on a trial basis. If the officers and I suspect foul play or manipulation taking place, or find that this competition starts any conflict between its members or officers, we will cancel it.

This is intended to be a fun way to motivate you all to become better and more active players. So let’s please keep this as clean and fun as possible and we hope that you all enjoy it!
